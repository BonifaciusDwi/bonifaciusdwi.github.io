About me

I am a 3rd Year UCU student studying GIS and interested in human-nature interactions

Background

I was born in Jakarta, Indonesia, but my family moved to Singapore when I was very young. I spent most of my childhood and teenage years in Singapore, where I attended school and made many friends. After completing my IB diploma, I decided to study Liberal Arts and Sciences at UCU.

Padi Diver

I have always been fascinated by the ocean and its many mysteries. When I turned 18, I decided to take up scuba diving as a hobby, and I quickly fell in love with it. Over the years, I have completed several diving courses, including the Padi Rescue Diver course, which taught me valuable skills for handling emergency situations underwater.

Wilderness experience

I have always enjoyed spending time outdoors and learning new skills. I have participated in several camping trips and survival courses, which have taught me how to learn and connect to nature in the wilderness.

Science major

I decided to major in Physics and Earth and Environment. My studies have taught me about the fundamental laws of nature, as well as the complex interplay between the Earth's various systems. I am excited to continue learning and contributing to our understanding of the world around us.
