Stop Ecocide International Blog Post

Overview

I co-authored a blog post introducing the collaboration between Youth for Ecocide Law and GARN Youth Hub, highlighting the need to transform environmental law through ecocide criminalisation and rights of nature.

Key Contributions

  • Simplified complex legal concepts into an accessible, compelling narrative.
  • Amplified youth voices and promoted intergenerational justice.
  • Strengthened brand positioning and community engagement ahead of COP30.

Impact

Increased awareness of the partnership, sparked dialogue on legal reform, and reinforced both organisations as leaders in conservation advocacy.

Ecocide In Our Eyes

I worked as a contributor to this book. A collection of art from young people around the world. It captures both the beauty of nature and the world around us as well as the pain and difficult emotions by seeing the mass destruction of nature that threatens our future. A particular focus of this book are art narratives and stories relating to the ocean. The book is launched at the 2025 UN Ocean Conference.

Geospatial tool Guest Lecture and Demonstration

Overview
